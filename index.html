ABOUT US

About The Borneo Coco

It can be briefly interpreted that we are a company that provides raw materials that will later be produced into a coconut derivative product by a factory, and we are also engaged in the distribution of coconut derivative products from our partners.

Our Products

Our products for a while that we are ready to offer there are 3, namely VCO, Charcoal from Coconut Shell, and Semi Husked Coconut.

History About Our Company

At first we were only suppliers of raw materials for products made from coconut, but over time we expanded our business to national and international-export, namely coconut processed products, including Virgin Coconut Oil, Charcoal and Coconut Fruit.
